Character Backstory

Born under the twin moons of Mystara, Elyndria showed magical aptitude from an early age. She apprenticed under the legendary Archmagus Zephyrian for a century before establishing her own tower in the misty peaks of the Silvercrests.

Now over 300 years old, she has mastered the elemental forces and seeks knowledge of the ancient magics lost to time.
